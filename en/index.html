    First AI-Powered software for Crop Disease Detection in Real Time

    What we do:
    CropVision provides cutting-edge agricultural technology that combines artificial intelligence with real-time image analysis to detect crop diseases before they spread. Unlike generic farming apps, our platform is uniquely tailored to serve local farmers with multilingual support, including Arabic and English, and accepts inputs from drones, smartphones, and remotely operated vehicles (ROVs). By offering instant disease diagnosis and tailored treatment suggestions, CropVision helps farmers prevent large-scale crop damage, reduce pesticide misuse, and optimize harvest yields. Our solution stands out by making advanced technology accessible, affordable, and practical for everyday farming operations—bridging the gap between innovation and hands-on agriculture.

    Key Features:

    • Real-Time Disease Detection
    • Arabic & English Interface
    • Drone, ROV, and Phone Image Support
    • Treatment Recommendations
    • Dashboard & Reports

    How It Works:

    • Capture or upload crop images
    • AI scans and analyzes the data
    • You receive disease reports and treatment tips
