    Our Mission:

    CropVision’s mission is to empower farmers with accessible AI technology that detects crop diseases early, improves yields, and reduces waste. We bridge the gap between innovation and fieldwork by providing real-time insights through drones, smartphones, and multilingual support. Our goal is to make smart farming practical, sustainable, and inclusive for all.

    Our Vision:

    Our vision is to lead the transformation of agriculture by making AI powered disease detection accessible to every farmer. We aim to create a future where technology enhances crop health, maximizes yields, and ensures food security worldwide, making sustainable farming practices the norm.

    Our Story:

    In the fertile lands of South Lebanon, farming is more than just a job—it’s a deep-rooted tradition passed down through generations. We’ve witnessed the hard work and dedication of our community’s farmers, who pour their hearts into their crops. However, despite their efforts, crop diseases, pests, and unpredictable weather often threaten their hard work, causing devastating losses. Inspired by these challenges, we created CropVision to help farmers detect crop diseases early using AI-powered technology. Our platform is designed to be accessible and practical for all farmers, enabling them to protect their crops and ensure a successful harvest. With CropVision, we hope to support our community and other farmers around the world, bridging the gap between tradition and innovation for a more sustainable future.
